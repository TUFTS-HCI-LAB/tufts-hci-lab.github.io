## Contact Us
### PhD Students
At this time, the HCI Lab is not accepting PhD Students 
### Undergraduate Students
To join the lab as a research assistant, current undergraduate students should contact hci@tufts.edu. Students will be asked to meet with one of the current PhD students to discuss their background, interests, and ensure their understanding of the lab's research. Alternatively, students can come to the open lab meeting at 2pm every Wednesday in the HCI Lab to schedule a time with the PhD Students.
